Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026  ·  Last Updated: June 21, 2026

Important: This Privacy Policy applies to information collected through our website (crescentmedicalri.com). Your medical records and protected health information (PHI) are governed separately by our HIPAA Notice of Privacy Practices, which is provided to you at your first visit and available at our front desk.

1. Who We Are

Crescent Medical Center LLC is a licensed primary care practice in the State of Rhode Island, with our main clinic in Johnston, RI and an additional location in North Smithfield. We are committed to protecting the privacy of everyone who visits our website or contacts us through it.

2. Information We Collect

When you use our website or communicate with our office, we may collect the following types of information:

  • Contact form submissions: Name, phone number, email address, and any message you provide when requesting an appointment or sending an inquiry.
  • New patient intake forms: Personal and health information submitted via our printed/PDF intake packet, used solely to prepare for your visit. This information is provided in person or by fax.
  • Mobile phone number and SMS preferences: If you provide a mobile number, we may use it to send appointment reminders and administrative text messages. See our Terms of Service for full SMS terms.
  • Usage data: General analytics such as pages visited, time on site, and browser type, collected through Google Analytics. This data is aggregated and not personally identifiable.

We do not collect payment information through this website. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information — including mobile phone numbers or SMS opt-in data — with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through this website is used only to:

  • Respond to appointment requests and general inquiries
  • Prepare for your first visit (intake forms)
  • Improve our website experience (analytics)
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

4. HIPAA & Protected Health Information

Crescent Medical Center is a HIPAA-covered entity. Any protected health information (PHI) you share with our clinical team — including information exchanged during appointments, via our patient portal (FollowMyHealth), or by fax — is handled in strict accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA).

Please note: Our website contact form, email, and SMS text messages are not HIPAA-secure channels. Do not include medical details, symptoms, or other PHI in website forms or text messages. New patient intake forms should be completed and either brought to your appointment in person or faxed to (401) 226-0854. For clinical communications, please use our patient portal or call us directly.

5. Cookies & Analytics

Our website uses Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. Google Analytics collects anonymized data including pages viewed, session duration, and general location (city/region level). No personally identifiable information is included. You may opt out of Google Analytics tracking by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

We do not use advertising cookies or share tracking data with third-party advertisers.

6. Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Formspree: Processes appointment request and contact form submissions. Data is transmitted securely. View Formspree's Privacy Policy.
  • FollowMyHealth (Patient Portal): An independent, HIPAA-compliant patient portal. View FollowMyHealth's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Analytics: Website usage analytics. View Google's Privacy Policy.

7. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained for up to 12 months for the purpose of coordinating care. Analytics data is retained per Google Analytics' standard policies. Medical records are retained in accordance with Rhode Island state law (minimum 10 years for adult patients).

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your contact/inquiry data (note: medical records are subject to separate retention requirements)
  • Opt out of analytics tracking (see Section 5)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at cmcpatientbookings@gmail.com or call (401) 227-9036 · (401) 284-7900.

9. Children's Privacy

Crescent Medical Center serves adults 18 and older. Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from minors through this website.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
